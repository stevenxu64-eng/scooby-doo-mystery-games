Scooby-Doo Mystery Games

Two fan-made browser mysteries. No installs, no plugins — just meddling kids, a hungry Great Dane, and villains who would've gotten away with it.

Episode 1 · Point-and-Click

The Curse of the Abandoned Resort

A classic screen-by-screen adventure. Search the derelict Grand Palm Resort, combine items, interrogate suspects, switch between Velma, Shaggy & Scooby, and Daphne — then rig a trap and accuse the right culprit.

Mouse to explore · arrows/WASD to move between rooms · 1–9 for dialogue

Night at the Spooky Museum
